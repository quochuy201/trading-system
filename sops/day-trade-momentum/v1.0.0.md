# Day Trade Momentum Strategy — v1.0.0

## Overview

Intraday momentum strategy targeting US equities with strong directional moves driven by **specific catalysts** and **abnormal volume**. The system adapts aggressiveness based on account size.

---

## Account Tiers

| Tier | Equity | Mode | Max Trades/Day | Max Position Size |
|------|--------|------|----------------|-------------------|
| Micro | < $25K | Selective | 1-2 | 5% of equity |
| Growth | $25K–$100K | Selective | 2-3 | 8% of equity |
| Standard | $100K–$250K | Hybrid | 3-5 | 10% of equity |
| Pro | > $250K | Hybrid | 5-8 | 10% of equity |

**Selective mode:** Only take A+ setups (score ≥ 80). Fewer trades, higher conviction.
**Hybrid mode:** Take A+ setups full size, B+ setups (score ≥ 70) at half size. Scale out at multiple targets.

## Phase 1: Pre-Market Scan (8:00–9:30 AM ET)

### Scan Criteria — ALL must be true:

| Criterion | Threshold | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Pre-market gap | > 3% (up or down) | Indicates overnight catalyst |
| Relative volume (RVOL) | > 2x average | Confirms institutional interest |
| Identifiable catalyst | Required (see below) | "It's going up" is NOT a catalyst |
| Price range | $10–$500 | Enough liquidity, not penny stock |
| Average daily volume | > 2M shares | Can exit without slippage |
| Float | > 20M shares | Avoids low-float squeezes (unpredictable) |

### Valid Catalysts (must classify as one):

| Catalyst Type | Example | Quality |
|---------------|---------|---------|
| EARNINGS_BEAT | Beat EPS + raised guidance | A+ |
| EARNINGS_MISS | Missed + gap down (for shorts) | A+ |
| SECTOR_EVENT | Oil crash → airlines rally | A |
| PRODUCT_LAUNCH | FDA approval, major contract | A |
| UPGRADE/DOWNGRADE | Major analyst PT change > 10% | B+ |
| MACRO_SHIFT | Fed decision, CPI surprise | B |
| TECHNICAL_BREAKOUT | Multi-month range breakout on volume | B |

**REJECT if:** No classifiable catalyst, "momentum play", "looks like it's going up", or stale catalyst (> 1 day old).

## Phase 2: Due Diligence (per candidate)

Before entering ANY position, verify all 5 checkpoints:

### DD Checkpoint 1: Catalyst Freshness
- Is the catalyst from TODAY or last night? (Day 1 only)
- Day 2+ catalysts are stale → **REJECT**
- Exception: multi-day sector rotation with new confirming data

### DD Checkpoint 2: Price Action Confirmation
Wait for the first 15 minutes (9:30–9:45 AM) to confirm direction:
- **Bull setup:** Higher highs AND higher lows in first 3+ bars after open
- **Bear setup:** Lower highs AND lower lows in first 3+ bars
- **REJECT if:** Immediate reversal of gap direction (gap trap)
- **REJECT if:** Gap > 15% (too extended, mean-reversion risk)

### DD Checkpoint 3: Volume Confirmation
- Volume in first 15 min should be > 3x average for that time period
- Volume should be INCREASING on moves in your direction
- **REJECT if:** Price moving on declining volume (distribution/accumulation trap)

### DD Checkpoint 4: Structure & Levels
- Identify nearest support (for longs) or resistance (for shorts)
- Entry must be within 1% of a structural level (not chasing)
- Stop must be below a structural level (not arbitrary)
- **REJECT if:** No clear structure (choppy, no defined levels)

### DD Checkpoint 5: Risk/Reward
- Minimum R:R of 2:1 (target must be 2x the distance to stop)
- Calculate using structural levels, not arbitrary percentages
- **REJECT if:** R:R < 2:1 or target requires breaking major resistance

### Due Diligence Verdict

| Checkpoints Passed | Verdict |
|-------------------|---------|
| 5/5 | ENTER |
| 4/5 | ENTER with half size |
| 3/5 or fewer | REJECT |

## Phase 3: Entry Rules

### Timing Window
- **Primary window:** 9:45 AM – 11:00 AM ET (highest momentum)
- **Secondary window:** 11:00 AM – 11:30 AM ET (only A+ setups)
- **NO entries after 11:30 AM ET** — momentum fades, risk of afternoon reversal

### Entry Method
1. **Wait for pullback to structure** — never chase
2. **Limit order at structural support** (for longs) or resistance (for shorts)
3. **Market order ONLY if:** Score ≥ 80 AND RVOL > 3x AND pullback is happening NOW
4. **Confirmation bar:** Entry bar must close in your direction with above-average volume

### Position Sizing

```
risk_per_trade = account_equity × tier_risk_pct
quantity = risk_per_trade / (entry_price - stop_loss)
position_value = quantity × entry_price
```

| Tier | Risk % per trade | Max position % |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| Micro | 0.5% | 5% |
| Growth | 1.0% | 8% |
| Standard | 1.0% | 10% |
| Pro | 0.75% | 10% |

### Entry Scoring (0–100)

| Signal | Weight | Full (100) | Half (50) | Zero (0) |
|--------|--------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Catalyst quality | 25% | A+ catalyst, Day 1 | B+ catalyst | No catalyst or stale |
| RVOL | 20% | > 3x | 2–3x | < 2x |
| Price action | 20% | Higher highs/lows, pullback to support | Trending but extended | Choppy or reversing |
| RSI(14) | 15% | 40–60 (not overbought) | 60–70 | > 75 or < 25 |
| Volume trend | 10% | Increasing on moves | Flat | Declining |
| R:R ratio | 10% | > 3:1 | 2–3:1 | < 2:1 |

**Minimum to trade:**
- Selective mode: Score ≥ 80
- Hybrid mode: Score ≥ 70 (half size for 70–79)

## Phase 4: Exit Strategy

### Exit Hierarchy (check in order, first trigger wins)

| Priority | Exit Type | Condition | Action |
|----------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Hard stop | Price hits stop_loss | Market sell immediately |
| 2 | Structural break | Closes below key support (longs) | Market sell |
| 3 | Volume death | Volume drops to < 30% of peak session volume | Tighten stop to breakeven |
| 4 | Take profit (full) | Price hits 2R target | Sell 100% (Selective) or 50% (Hybrid) |
| 5 | Trailing stop | After 1R profit, trail by 1× ATR | Sell on trail hit |
| 6 | Time stop | 3:45 PM ET | Close ALL remaining positions |

### Stop-Loss Rules

**Initial stop placement:**
- Below the nearest structural support (for longs)
- OR 1.5× ATR below entry (whichever is tighter)
- Never wider than 3% from entry

**Stop management:**
1. **Never move stop DOWN** (for longs) — this is non-negotiable
2. **Breakeven:** Move stop to entry price after +1R unrealized
3. **Trail:** After +1.5R, trail by 1× ATR (recalculated each bar)
4. **Tighten on volume death:** If volume drops to 30% of session peak, move stop to breakeven regardless of P&L

### Trailing Stop Logic (Hybrid mode — scale out)

| Profit Level | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| +1R | Move stop to breakeven. Sell 25% at market. |
| +1.5R | Trail remaining by 1× ATR |
| +2R | Sell another 25% (take profit). Trail rest by 0.75× ATR |
| +3R | Sell another 25%. Trail final 25% by 0.5× ATR |
| Trail hit | Sell all remaining |

### Anti-Panic Rules

These rules PREVENT premature exits:

1. **No selling above stop unless a structural break occurs.** Feeling scared is not a reason to sell.
2. **Volume death = tighten, not exit.** Move to breakeven and let the trade play out.
3. **Red candle ≠ exit signal.** Only exit on CLOSE below support, not intraday wick.
4. **If in doubt, reduce size (sell half) rather than full exit.** Preserves upside.
5. **Never exit in the first 5 minutes of holding** unless hard stop is hit. Give the trade time to work.

## Phase 5: Daily Risk Limits

| Rule | Condition | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Daily loss limit | P&L < -2% of equity (Micro/Growth) or -3% (Standard/Pro) | Stop trading for the day |
| Consecutive losses | 3 losses in a row | Pause 30 min, reduce next trade to half size |
| Win streak | 3 wins in a row | Normal size (don't get greedy) |
| Kill switch | Triggered by system | Close all immediately |
| Max daily trades | Per tier limit | Stop entering, only manage existing |

---

## Phase 6: End-of-Day Review

At 4:00 PM ET, the orchestrator must:

1. Close any remaining positions (should be none if time stop worked)
2. Log all trades with entry/exit reasoning
3. Calculate daily metrics (P&L, win rate, avg R)
4. Flag any SOP violations
5. Update trailing performance metrics
6. Send daily summary notification

## Rule IDs (for decision logging)

Use these tags in `log_decision` → `rules_triggered`:

### Entry Rules
- `CATALYST_FRESH` — Day 1 catalyst confirmed
- `RVOL_HIGH` — Relative volume > 2x
- `PRICE_ACTION_CONFIRM` — Higher highs/lows confirmed
- `VOLUME_CONFIRM` — Volume increasing on moves
- `STRUCTURE_ENTRY` — Entry at structural level
- `RR_VALID` — Risk/reward ≥ 2:1
- `SCORE_PASS` — Overall score ≥ threshold

### Exit Rules
- `STOP_HIT` — Hard stop triggered
- `STRUCTURAL_BREAK` — Closed below key support
- `VOLUME_DEATH` — Volume collapsed, tightened to breakeven
- `TAKE_PROFIT` — Hit target price
- `TRAILING_STOP` — Trail level hit
- `TIME_STOP` — 3:45 PM mandatory close
- `SCALE_OUT` — Partial exit at profit level (Hybrid mode)

### Rejection Rules
- `NO_CATALYST` — No identifiable catalyst
- `STALE_CATALYST` — Catalyst > 1 day old
- `GAP_TRAP` — Gap reversed immediately
- `GAP_TOO_LARGE` — Gap > 15%
- `VOLUME_DECLINING` — Volume dropping on move
- `NO_STRUCTURE` — No clear support/resistance
- `RR_INSUFFICIENT` — R:R < 2:1
- `SCORE_FAIL` — Score below threshold
- `DAILY_LIMIT` — Daily loss limit reached
- `MAX_TRADES` — Max trades for the day reached
- `TIME_WINDOW_CLOSED` — After 11:30 AM ET
