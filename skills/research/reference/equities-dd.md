# Equities Due Diligence — Reference

## Scanning Criteria (Day Trade / Swing)

| Criterion | Day Trade | Swing |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Avg volume (20d) | > 1M shares | > 500K shares |
| Price range | $5 — $500 | $10 — $500 |
| Gap / momentum | Gap > 2% OR intraday > 1.5% | Weekly move > 3% with volume |
| Earnings | No earnings today | No earnings within 3 days |
| Float | > 20M (avoid low-float traps) | > 50M |

## Scoring Rubric (Day Trade)

| Signal | Weight | Bullish (full) | Neutral (half) | Bearish (zero) |
|--------|--------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| RSI(14) | 20% | 30–50 (bounce) | 50–65 | > 80 or < 20 |
| MACD | 20% | Bullish crossover, histogram rising | Flat | Bearish crossover |
| Volume ratio | 20% | > 1.5x avg | 1.0–1.5x | < 0.7x |
| Price vs SMA(20) | 15% | Above, SMA trending up | At SMA | Below |
| ATR expansion | 10% | ATR > 1.2x its 20d avg | Normal | Contracting |
| Catalyst/News | 15% | Fresh positive catalyst | No news | Negative |

**Threshold: 65 to trade. 80+ = strong conviction.**

## Entry Triggers (Don't Chase)

Best entries are NOT the first excitement candle. Wait for:
- **Breakout + hold**: Price breaks level, pulls back, holds above → enter
- **VWAP reclaim**: Price dips below VWAP, reclaims with volume → enter
- **Pullback to support**: Price pulls back to SMA20 or prior breakout level → enter
- **Opening range breakout**: First 15-min range breaks with volume → enter

**Never enter:** Extended moves (> 2 ATR from open), low volume breakouts, counter-trend.

## Exit Framework

| Type | Day Trade | Swing |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Take-profit | 2:1 R/R | 3:1 R/R |
| Stop-loss | Below support or 1.5× ATR | Below SMA20 or swing low |
| Trailing | After 1R: trail by 1 ATR | After 2R: trail by SMA20 |
| Time stop | 3:45 PM ET (mandatory) | 5 days max hold without progress |

## Red Flags (Auto-Reject)

- Stock below SMA200 (for longs) — fighting the primary trend
- Gap > 15% at open — wait until 10:30 ET, skip if fading
- Penny stock (< $5) or micro-cap (< $300M market cap)
- Meme stock with no fundamental catalyst
- Earnings within 1 day (binary event, not a setup)
- Spread > 0.5% (illiquid, bad fills)
